Welcome to ThyroVaidya

The ThyroVaidya is an innovative digital platform designed to empower users in managing their thyroid health. Our solution allows users to assess their risk of thyroid disease by inputting symptoms, resulting in a prediction categorized as high, medium, or low risk. For those with thyroid test results, our model analyzes T3 and T4 levels to identify specific thyroid disorders and provides personalized dietary and exercise recommendations. Registered users benefit from a personalized dashboard to book appointments with thyroid specialists and hospitals in the Kolhapur region. The platform also features educational resources on thyroid disease, including its types, symptoms, and treatments, complemented by an interactive 3D model of the thyroid gland. Designed with a user-friendly interface, the Thyroid Health Assistant combines predictive analytics and personalized support, making it a unique tool for proactive thyroid health management. Our focus on accessibility, early detection, and informed self-care makes this platform an invaluable resource for individuals seeking to improve their thyroid health.
